Privacy Policy for Wade

Last Updated: June 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how La Fosse Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you play Wade (the "Game") on the Meta Quest platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

When you play Wade, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: We receive basic information about your Meta account, such as your Meta ID, username, and profile picture, which is necessary for you to access and play the Game. We do not collect your email address or full name directly from Meta.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your Meta Quest device, including its hardware specifications (e.g., model), operating system, and unique device identifiers. This helps us optimize the Game for your device and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • Usage Data: We collect data related to how you interact with the Game. This includes:
    • Gameplay Data: Your progress within the game, scores, achievements, in-game purchases, and how often you play.
    • Interaction Data: Information about how you interact with in-game menus, characters, objects, and features.
    • Crash Reports: If the Game crashes, we may collect anonymized crash reports to help us identify and fix bugs.
  • Content Interactions: We may collect information about your interactions within those features, such as the content you view or interact with. We do not store the content of your voice chats or messages.
  • Payment Information: If you make in-game purchases, your payment information (e.g., credit card details) is processed directly by Meta and/or their third-party payment processors. We do not collect or store your sensitive payment information. We only receive confirmation of your purchase.
  • Communications: If you contact our support team, we will collect the information you provide in your communication to address your inquiries and provide assistance.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing and Improving Services: To operate, maintain, and improve the Game's functionality, content, and user experience. This includes delivering updates, bug fixes, and new features.
  • Personalization: To tailor your in-game experience, such as offering personalized content recommendations (e.g., levels, items) based on your gameplay history and preferences.
  • Safety and Security: To help maintain the security and integrity of the Game, detect and prevent fraudulent activity, and protect our users.
  • Communication: To communicate with you about your account, in-game purchases, and important updates regarding the Game. We may also send you optional push notifications if you enable them on your device.
  • Research and Development: We may use aggregated and anonymized data for research and analytical purposes to understand user trends, improve our game design, and develop new features. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We are committed to minimizing data sharing. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency request) or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.

4. User Rights and Controls

You have certain rights regarding your personal data. Please note that some rights may be subject to limitations based on technical feasibility or legal requirements:

  • Access and Correction: You can access and update certain information associated with your Meta account directly through your Meta Quest device settings. For any game-specific data that is not accessible through Meta's tools, please contact us using the information below.
  • Deletion: You may be able to request the deletion of certain data associated with your Game progress by contacting us. Please note that deleting your data may result in the loss of your game progress and achievements. Meta also provides tools to delete your Meta account and associated data.
  • Privacy Settings: You have control over various privacy settings on your Meta Quest device, including:
    • Microphone Access: You can enable or disable microphone access for the Game through your device settings.
    • Camera and Passthrough Access: You can manage access to your device's camera for Passthrough features.
    • Eye Tracking and Facial Expressions: You can enable or disable these features specifically for our Game within your Meta Quest device settings.
    • Notifications: You can manage push notifications from the Game through your device settings.
  • Consent Management: For features like eye tracking and facial expressions, your explicit consent is required through your Meta Quest device settings before we can access such data. You can withdraw your consent at any time by disabling these features in your device settings.

5. Legal Compliance

We are committed to complying with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents, and other relevant data protection laws.

We strive to provide clear and concise information about our data collection, usage, and sharing practices in this Privacy Policy.

6. Additional Considerations

  • Children's Privacy: Our Game is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information as quickly as possible. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's online activities and to help enforce our Privacy Policy by instructing their children never to provide personal information through the Game without their permission.
  • Data Retention: We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide you with the Game and its features, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. The specific retention period will depend on the type of data and the purpose for which it was collected. For instance, gameplay progress data may be retained as long as you have an active account, while anonymized analytics data may be retained for longer periods for research and improvement purposes.
  • Changes to this Privacy Policy: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on the Meta Store page for our Game and/or through in-game notifications. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the Game after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

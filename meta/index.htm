Privacy Policy for Wade

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how La Fosse Studio ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you play Wade (the "Game") on the Meta Quest platform. We are committed to user privacy and transparency.

Data Privacy Commitment: Wade is designed to be a private experience. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, gameplay analytics, or sensitive information to any external servers.

1. Information Collection and Usage

Because we do not use external servers or tracking SDKs, our data footprint is minimal:

  • Platform Data: The Game may access your Meta User ID and Username locally on your device to facilitate basic platform functionality (such as displaying your name in the game menu). This information stays on your device and is never sent to us.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics, Unity Analytics, or GameAnalytics). Your gameplay patterns and behaviors are not tracked.
  • Hardware Features: If the Game utilizes microphone or passthrough features, all processing happens locally on your Meta Quest headset. We do not record, store, or transmit any audio or visual data from your environment.

2. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, we do not share, sell, or trade any user information with third parties.

3. Data Deletion

Because Wade does not store any user data on its own servers, there is no personal data in our possession to delete. All game progress is stored locally on your device or via Meta's integrated cloud storage.

If you have any questions regarding your privacy or wish to confirm that no data has been stored, please contact us at wadevr-support@gmail.com. We will respond to and process all privacy-related requests within 30 days.

4. User Controls

You can manage permissions (Microphone, Spatial Data, etc.) at any time through your Meta Quest device settings. Note that Meta (the platform provider) may collect its own data according to the Meta Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for a general audience. Because we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect or store data from children under the age of 13.
